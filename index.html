Quantum Concepts In Depth

Interactive exploration of quantum physics with Input, Process, and Output for each concept

About This Project

This repository provides in-depth, interactive implementations of quantum physics concepts. Each concept is presented with a structured approach:

Input: Interactive controls to adjust parameters and explore different scenarios
Process: Step-by-step explanations of the quantum mechanics and mathematics involved
Output: Real-time 3D visualizations and graphs showing the results

Click on any concept below to explore it in depth. Each page is standalone and includes interactive controls, detailed explanations, and beautiful visualizations to help you understand quantum physics intuitively.
